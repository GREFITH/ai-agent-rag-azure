COMPANY POLICIES AND GUIDELINES
================================

1. LEAVE POLICY

Annual Leave:
- Full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year
- Leave accrues at 1.67 days per month
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to 5 days into the next year
- Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance

Sick Leave:
- Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- Medical certificate required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days
- Sick leave does not carry forward to the next year

2. REMOTE WORK POLICY

Eligibility:
- Available to employees who have completed probation period
- Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Must maintain core hours of 10 AM - 3 PM
- Required to attend in-person meetings when scheduled

Equipment:
- Company provides laptop and necessary software
- Home internet costs reimbursed up to $50/month

3. WORKING HOURS

Standard Hours:
- Full-time: 40 hours per week
- Flexible start time between 7 AM - 10 AM
- Core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM (must be available)

4. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Training Budget:
- $2,000 per employee per year
- Can be used for courses, conferences, certifications
